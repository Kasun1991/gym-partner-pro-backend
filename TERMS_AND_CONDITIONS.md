## 2. What the App Does

Gym Partner Pro is a tool for managing gym members. It lets you:

- Record member profiles (name, identity number, contact details, address, blood type, birthday, age, email, profile photo)
- Record member medical information (medical conditions, allergies, blood pressure, notes)
- Record emergency contacts for each member
- Track gym subscriptions, plan durations, prices, start and end dates, freeze status and lifecycle status
- Track body measurements (weight, height, BMI, body fat percentage, chest, waist, hips, arms)
- Send SMS renewal reminders by opening your device's SMS app with a pre-filled message (the App itself does not send SMS — your device's SMS app does, after you confirm)
- Receive local notifications for birthdays, expiry reminders, and SMS reminder prompts
- Optionally back up data to **Firebase Firestore** under your own Google account when you sign in and turn on cloud sync

The App stores data **locally** on your device using an SQLite database. Cloud sync is optional and off by default.
