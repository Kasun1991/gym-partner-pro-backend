Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This policy explains what data Gym Partner Pro collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Member data you enter: names, contact details, body measurements, membership and subscription records. This data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers without your explicit opt-in.

Crash reports: If you have not opted out, Firebase Crashlytics collects anonymous crash logs to help us improve stability. No personally identifiable information is included.

Analytics: Firebase Analytics collects anonymised usage events (screen views, feature taps) to help us understand how the app is used. This data cannot be linked back to you.

2. Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you sign in with your Google account, your member data is backed up to Cloud Firestore in your Firebase project. You can sign out and delete this data at any time from within the app.

3. Advertising

The free tier displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google may use device identifiers for ad personalisation. You can opt out via your device's ad settings.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — crash reporting, analytics, optional sync
  • Google AdMob — advertising on free tier
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All local data is deleted when you uninstall the app. Cloud backup data can be deleted by signing out and tapping "Delete cloud data" in Settings. To request deletion of any data held by our analytics providers, follow the respective provider's opt-out process.

6. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy? Use the Contact Support form in the app.
